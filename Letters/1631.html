Landau, 8.9.17

Dear Elsa!

I have thankfully gotten your dear letter. Hopefully, you too got the letter that I sent to you last week on Friday? Yesterday, a non-commissioned medic officer was needed for a field hospital, and I right away volunteered, because up until now, every week 1 - 2 non-commissioned medic officers are sent out on the field, and I would have to be in line soon anyway, to go into the field; I thought to myself, now it’s time to make yourself safe. As a paramedic in the trenches nothing is certain. One has it as good in a field hospital as in a war hospital. Maybe at the end of next week we’ll move out, to Romania in fact. It’s going to be a nice trip, one gets to see another piece of the world.

I will be trying to get a vacation to Ludwigshafen, since I want to see you one more time before I go away. Who knows if I will come back from there, to the homeland.

Please write to me again before-hand, dear Elsa!

Greetings from the heart until then,

From your friend Tony.

Also, greetings from the heart to Mother, Grandmother and Erna.
